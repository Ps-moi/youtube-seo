# Munchkin Cat × Birds × Nature — AI Image Prompt Generator

## Purpose
Generate a **series of diverse, photorealistic AI image prompts** featuring a short-haired
Munchkin cat as the main subject, surrounded by birds, optional squirrels, food feeders,
toys, and lush nature environments. Each prompt must be a completely different scene.

---

## Core Subject Rules (NEVER change these)

### 🐱 The Cat — Short-haired Munchkin
- Breed: **Munchkin cat, short-haired** — explicitly state "short-haired Munchkin cat"
- Fur: smooth, flat, not fluffy or puffed
- Expression: **cute, sweet, cheerful, happy, playful** — NEVER cool/aloof/fierce/fierce-looking
- Posture: **sitting, crouching, pawing, tilting head, reaching, sniffing** — **NEVER lying down**
- The cat is always the **visual focal point** of the image
- Each prompt must give the cat a **different fur color/pattern**:
  - Examples: cream tabby, orange mackerel tabby, blue-grey solid, calico, chocolate point,
    lilac, black-and-white tuxedo, cinnamon, silver spotted tabby, golden shaded

### 🐦 Approved Bird Species (use ONLY these 4, each a different color)
| Bird | Color |
|------|-------|
| Cardinalis cardinalis (Northern Cardinal) | vibrant red |
| Baltimore/Altamira Oriole | bright orange |
| Cyanistes caeruleus (Eurasian Blue Tit) | blue and yellow |
| White-rumped Shama | black, white, and chestnut |

- **NO fish-eating birds** (no herons, kingfishers, pelicans, etc.)
- Bird count per prompt follows the series rule (see Series Structure below)
- Each bird should have a distinct, visible action (perching, eating, singing, landing, hopping)
- Birds **add visual vibrancy** and complement — they do NOT overshadow the cat

---

## Series Structure

The default series is **5 prompts** with this bird/squirrel count:

| Prompt | Birds | Squirrel |
|--------|-------|----------|
| 1 | 2 | 1 squirrel |
| 2 | 2 | 1 squirrel |
| 3 | 3 | none |
| 4 | 3 | none |
| 5 | 3 | 1 squirrel |

> The user may request custom counts — adapt accordingly.

### Squirrel Rules
- Squirrel is **optional** per prompt (see table above)
- Squirrel may: peek from behind an object (rock, feeder, flower pot, log) OR stand openly
- Squirrel position: near the feeder OR near the birds — not isolated far from the scene
- Squirrel must NOT be larger than the cat

---

## Scene Construction Rules

### 🌿 Background — Grass is MANDATORY primary ground
> **Rule:** Lush green grass must be the dominant ground cover. Secondary elements
> (soil patches, fine sand, stone path) are allowed but must be minor.

Build scenes in layers for **depth of field**:
1. **Foreground**: some grass blades, a small flower or pebble — slightly soft
2. **Midground**: cat + feeder + birds (sharp focus)
3. **Background**: garden feature, trees, shrubs, fence, etc. (soft blur, not heavy)

### 🌸 Flowers
- Include flowers **in moderation** — 1 small cluster or 2–3 individual blooms is enough
- NO overwhelming flower fields that distract from the cat
- Choose flowers appropriate to the scene season/region
- Examples: astilbe, lavender, chamomile, cosmos, mini roses, wild daisies, clover blooms

### ☀️ Lighting
- **Always:** soft natural light (morning golden hour OR soft afternoon OR overcast diffused)
- **NEVER:** backlit / contre-jour / harsh midday overhead shadows
- Light must fall ON the cat's face — cheeks and eyes should be bright and visible

---

## Food Feeder Rules

### Container Style
Each prompt uses a **different feeder design** — must be cute, decorative, scene-appropriate:
- Hollowed bamboo joint
- Miniature ceramic bowl with hand-painted pattern
- Tiny wooden tray feeder on a stump
- Small woven rattan basket feeder
- Mini terracotta pot on a flat stone
- Rustic half coconut shell on a branch
- Tiny acorn-cap shaped ceramic dish
- Small glass dome feeder on a wooden stick

### Size Rule
> ⚠️ The feeder **must always be visibly smaller than the cat**. State this explicitly in the prompt.

### Food Contents — Minimum 4 types per prompt
Always include: **halved peas** (must be explicitly cut in half — "halved green peas")
Mix in at least 3 more from:
- Sunflower seeds
- Chopped almonds
- Dried cranberries
- Millet seeds
- Diced apple pieces
- Hulled oats
- Dried blueberries
- Small maize kernels
- Crushed walnuts
- Hemp seeds

## Cat Toy Rules (1 toy per prompt, vary each time)

Choose toys that make the scene feel natural and playful:
- A small feather wand lying nearby (cat has been batting it)
- A tiny crinkle ball near the cat's paws
- A colorful ribbon tied to a garden stake, fluttering
- A mini catnip sachet the cat is sniffing
- A small wooden ring toy the cat is pawing
- A soft plush bird toy (not a real bird) the cat is nudging
- A ping-pong ball the cat is nosing through the grass

> Never place the toy as the main focus — it's a **prop** that adds storytelling context.

## Background / Setting Variety
Each prompt must use a **different garden/nature setting**. Rotate through:
- Japanese bamboo water spout (shishi-odoshi) + stone basin
- Weathered wooden garden bench surrounded by greenery
- Old stone wall with climbing ivy
- Wooden picket fence with wildflowers
- Mossy garden stepping stones under a tree
- Rustic wooden birdhouse post in the garden
- Under a blooming cherry/plum tree
- Near a terracotta pot garden corner
- A sunlit meadow edge with a tree stump
- A gravel path garden with lavender borders

## Prompt Template

Use this structure to write each prompt. Fill all bracketed fields:

```
The image presents [SCENE MOOD + SETTING DESCRIPTION]. Primary ground is lush green grass
[+ minor secondary ground detail]. [FLOWER DETAIL — 1 cluster or few blooms nearby].
Style: Clear, well-lit [LIGHT DIRECTION] shot, detailed background with clear depth of field,
avoiding excessive blur.

Midground: A [CAT FUR COLOR/PATTERN], short-haired Munchkin cat with a sweet, happy,
adorably curious expression is [CAT POSTURE/ACTION — NOT lying down]. Nearby, a [FEEDER TYPE]
(noticeably smaller than the cat) contains [FOOD 1], [FOOD 2], [FOOD 3], [FOOD 4+],
and halved green peas.

[BIRD 1 NAME + COLOR] is [ACTION] on/near the feeder.
[BIRD 2 NAME + COLOR] is [ACTION] [LOCATION].
[BIRD 3 NAME + COLOR (if applicable)] is [ACTION] [LOCATION].

[SQUIRREL LINE if applicable: A small squirrel is [peeking from behind OBJECT / standing near
feeder], [expression/action].]

[CAT TOY LINE: A [TOY] lies near the cat's paws / is being batted by the cat.]

The scene is bathed in [LIGHT DESCRIPTION], strictly avoiding any backlight. The cat remains
the clear focal point, with birds adding colorful vibrancy to the composition.
```
